# Blackout is out

Blackout is a simple yet efficient application designed to allow users to quickly black out their screens in just a few seconds. Whether you need to take a break from your screen or to sneake on late :) 


## Features:
- **Quick Blackout:** Instantly black out your screen with a single command.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Designed for speed and ease of use.
- **Lightweight:** Minimal resource usage to enhance performance.

## Installation:
To use Blackout, simply download the app from the releases section on GitHub, and follow the installation instructions provided in the documentation.

## Usage:
1. Launch the app.
2. Click the "Toggle blackout" button or use the designated shortcut.
3. To restore the screen, simply click the repress the shortcut button.
